Sample Data Directory
===================

This directory is intended to store sample videos and images for testing the Traffic Management System.

Recommended sample files:
- traffic_video.mp4: Sample traffic video with vehicles and pedestrians
- intersection.jpg: Sample intersection image
- crosswalk.mp4: Video showing pedestrian crosswalk activity
- violation_example.mp4: Video with traffic violations for testing

You can add your own video/image files here for testing the application.

Supported formats:
- Videos: .mp4, .avi, .mov, .mkv
- Images: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .bmp

Note: The YOLOv8 model will be automatically downloaded when first run.
